CapaStr: Talking about design

Published: Tue 20 August 2013

In misc.

tags: Android CapaStr Cordova iOS JQueryMobile PhoneGap

It was very difficult for me to choose the best and easiest framework to use for UI design. After lot of research I decided to stick with **JQueryMobile** since it has an awesome extension called **ThemeRoller**. Working with a framework gives you enough time to work on core things rather than thinking about the responsive design. Cordova is doing pretty well with JQuery without loosing any frame.
