GSoC'14 progress: Week 6

Published: Wed 02 July 2014
By Sudhanshu Mishra (noreply@blogger.com)

In misc.

tags: gsoc physics sympy

It was a busy week though I managed to do some work.
I've been working on following things:
  • Integrating TWave with refraction_angle(7626)
  • Interference class
Some blocking 3D geometry code got merged yesterday which gives me a lot of new things to work on. :-)
I also tried to take a look on very old [patch](https://github.com/sympy/sympy/pull/1226) for `gaussopt`{style="background-color: #f7f7f9; border-bottom-left-radius: 3px; border-bottom-right-radius: 3px; border-top-left-radius: 3px; border-top-right-radius: 3px; border: 1px solid rgb(225, 225, 232); color: #dd1144; font-family: Consolas, 'Liberation Mono', Courier, monospace !important; font-size: 12px; padding: 3px 4px;"}.
This week I wrote a script to update the development documentation when a PR gets merged into the master. This is up and running.
I passed mid term evaluation that took place last week and I would like to thank [Sean](https://github.com/flacjacket), [Ondrej](https://github.com/certik),[Aaron](https://github.com/asmeurer) and [Chris](https://github.com/smichr) for their constant support.
That's all for now. *Cheers!*
