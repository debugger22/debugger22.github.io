GSoC‘14 progress: Week 6

Published: Wed 02 July 2014
By Sudhanshu Mishra

In GSoC.

tags: gsoc physics sympy

It was a busy week though I managed to do some work.

  • Integrating TWave with refraction_angle(7626)

That’s all for now. Cheers!
