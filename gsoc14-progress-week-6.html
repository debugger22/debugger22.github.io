  • Integrating TWave with refraction_angle(7626)
  • Interference class
Some blocking 3D geometry code got merged yesterday which gives me a lot of new things to work on. :-)
I also tried to take a look on very old [patch](https://github.com/sympy/sympy/pull/1226) for `gaussopt`{style="background-color: #f7f7f9; border-bottom-left-radius: 3px; border-bottom-right-radius: 3px; border-top-left-radius: 3px; border-top-right-radius: 3px; border: 1px solid rgb(225, 225, 232); color: #dd1144; font-family: Consolas, 'Liberation Mono', Courier, monospace !important; font-size: 12px; padding: 3px 4px;"}.

That's all for now. *Cheers!*
