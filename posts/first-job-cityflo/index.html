First Job

Published: Sun 17 January 2016

In Work.

tags: job python

It's been several months since my last post here. GSoC went pretty well this year. There are still a lot of work to be done in assumptions. Moreover, after finishing my five months long practice school at Ernst & Young LLP, Chennai I came back to campus for placements.

On 10th Jan 2016, I got selected in a company called Cityflo which is based out of Mumbai. Cityflo is an urban transportation startup focused on solving the daily commute problem using technology and data. My work over there will be involving primarily Python. I'm really excited about it.

On the other hand, this placement marks the end of my campus life at BITS. I'll be leaving for home in a couple of days. Graduating in 3.5 years wasn't really difficult. Besides, it leaves you with an extra 5 months in your life! I'll hopefully use it to explore some places before I join Cityflo in early March. I enjoyed my time at BITS and learnt invaluable lessons.

That's about me! I wish good luck to others for the placements and acads.
