GSoC'15: Fourth biweekly update

Published: Tue 14 July 2015

In GSoC.

tags: python sympy gsoc

During this period we've been able to finish and merge following PRs:

  • Documenting new assumptions
  • Deprecate Q.infinitesimal predicate
  • Remove obsolete doctest skip from nonzero predicate
