Some Ideas

// under

There are several basic things where computer vision can be applied to make things easier by increasing automation. Some fields which I can figure out are:     **1.** Collecting data from CCTVs installed in public places and making some sense out of it by finding patterns and activities that people usually do to generate some revenue or making use of that data for development of the country.     This is only possible by mastering BigData and computer vision.       **    2.** Detecting presence of people in a room to save electricity and switching appliances on/off by observing their gesture and speech.        Later on it can be converted into a device for people.
